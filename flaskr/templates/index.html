Welcome to Humboldt, a tool for large-scale sociolinguistics for everyone!

We provide an interface to search lexical phenomena in several languages, and to get statistical analysis and maps of the results along several demographic factors. And the best: You don't need any programming experience, training in statistical methods, knowledge of geographical information systems, or expertise in natural language processing.

This tool is named after the Humboldt brothers, because - like them - it combines linguistic knowledge and scientific exploration. We use large amounts of data from online reviews.
